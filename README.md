- Bridgetek Eve2 product page: http://www.ftdichip.com/EVE.htm
- Support Forums: http://www.lcdforums.com/forums/viewforum.php?f=45

Supports
  EVE2 FT812 & FT813
  EVE3 BT815 & BT816
  EVE4 BT817 & BT818
